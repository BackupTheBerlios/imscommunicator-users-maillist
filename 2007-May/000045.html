Hi.

Our normal experience with IMS-Communicator at initial registering was that the
username given thru keyboard was overriden and taken from xml file, and only
the given password was taken into account.
But let's say that a user has one private identity ("dave") and two public ones
("dave" and "david"). If the user wants to use his second publicID ("david"),
he has to follow a weird process to achieve that (you can follow it in the
attached capture):

1st try: Give a false password, and press Login.
2nd try: Give the true password and the second publicID ("david"), and press
Login.
3rd try: Give the true password and the second publicID ("david"), and press
Login, once again.

Is there any reasoning for this behaviour? Is it anywhere documented?

Thanks in advance.
