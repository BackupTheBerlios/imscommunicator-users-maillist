Hi all IMS-Communiator users and developers, 
There's an issue when trying to authenticate IMS-Communicator release 070427 against the OpenIMSCore r261 and up.
This is must probably due to an IPSec establishment problem between the IMS-Communicator (which does not yet set-up a SA using IPSec) and the latest P-CSCF.
Since both IMS-Communicator and OpenIMSCore are still work in progress, these situations are expected to happen. We want to express our gratitude for those who have been patient and help us improving this open-source project.
We hope to be able to provide a new release soon issuing this problem.
