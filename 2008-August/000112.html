Hi,

I have made a test-call between Bria(Alice) and IMS-communicator(Bob) and
detected a violation of SDP offer/answer model in IMS-communicator. The
INVITE sent by Bria did not contain a "m=video" line in SDP offer but the
200 OK sent by IMS-communicator included one in SDP answer. As a consequence
Bria immediately closes the session.

See the tracefile and szenario attached (SDP-issue.zip).

Background: 
RFC 3264 states in §6: The answer MUST contain exactly the same number of
"m=" lines as the offer.
