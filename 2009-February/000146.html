Hi maanasa,
The IMS-Communicator currently only supports "peer-to-peer" presence.
Check the following properties:

-          net.java.sip.communicator.ENABLE_SIMPLE (true to enable presence and IM)

-          net.java.sip.communicator.sip.simple

o   CONTACT_LIST_FILE

o   SUBSCRIPTION_EXP_TIME

o   MIN_EXP_TIME

o   LAST_SELECTED_OPEN_STATUS
Let me know if you find any problem.
Good luck ;-)
