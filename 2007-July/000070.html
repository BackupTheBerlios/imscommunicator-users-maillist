And the log file does not include an atempt for establising a call so I don't see the exception "Couldn'tset any of the tracks to valid RTP format".
Is the other client also IMS-Communicator? The properties under "net.java.sip.communicator.media" in both ims-communicator.xml files are equal to each other?
At a certain point, there was a problem when trying to establish a session between two clients IMS-Communicator that originated that exception, but that was patched in the latest version available at berlios.de.
